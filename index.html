  1. 1 Brentwood Wi-Fi Connection Issue:
    • When I tried to connect to Brentwood's Wi-Fi, a pop-up message appeared asking me to log in. However, one of the buttons in the pop-up did not display any text, leaving me confused.   2. 2 Hinge Profile Scrolling Issue:
    • On Hinge, when I match with someone, their profile information is displayed in a section that requires horizontal scrolling to view more details. However, there is no clear indication or instruction that horizontal scrolling is needed. This could lead to new users not realizing they are missing additional information, resulting in a frustrating user experience.
